Tribute to The CDS Bipin Rawat

First CDS of INDIA / Ex-General of Indian Army

General Bipin Rawat is the first CDS(Chief of Defence Staff) of India, which means he was the Chief of all three wings of Indian Armed forces i.e. Army Navy and Air Force. He was boarn in Pauri town of Pauri Garhwal district, present-day Uttarakhand state on 16 march 1958. His familiy has been ssrving in the Indian Army for multiple generations. Bipin Rawat was commissioned into the 5th Battalion the 11 Gorkha Rifles (5/11 GR) on 16 December 1978, the same unit as his father. He had much experience in High-altitude warfare and spent ten years conducting counter-insercency operation. He commanded a company in Uri,Jammu and Kashmir as a Major ans as a Colonel, after promotion as brigadier he commanded 5 Sector of RR(Rashtriya Rifles) in Sopore.

CDS Bipin Rawat contributed in many of the missions held by in POK and Easter Borders also to eliminate terrirists over there. In 2015, eighteen Indian soldiers were killed in an ambush by militants belonging to the United Liberation Front of Western South East Asia(UNLFW) in Manipur. Indian Amry responded with cross border strikes in which unit of 21st battalion of the Parachute Regiment astruck an NSCN-k base in Myanmar. He became Chief of Aemy Staff on 16 December 2016, he was the 27th Chief of army.
